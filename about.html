About Us

Royal Properties has spent over a decade turning Kanchipuram's growth corridor into safe, well-documented investments for families and investors alike.

Our Story

Smart investment, secure future

Royal Properties was founded on a simple frustration: too many land deals in and around Kanchipuram fell apart over paperwork nobody had bothered to check first. We built our business around fixing that — verifying every title, every survey line and every approval before a listing ever reaches a buyer.

Today we work across residential plots, villas, farm lands, apartments and commercial spaces throughout the Chennai–Bengaluru growth belt, guiding first-time buyers and seasoned investors through every step from enquiry to registration.

Under the leadership of Managing Director E. Peter Partheepan, M.E., the team combines local market knowledge with in-house legal support — so what you sign is exactly what you were promised.
