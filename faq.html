FAQ

Questions we hear the most

Can't find your answer here? Reach out and we'll get back to you directly.

Are all your plots DTCP or RERA approved?+

Yes — every plot listing on our site carries a DTCP or RERA approval number, verified before it's published.

Do you assist with home loans?+

We work with partner banks and NBFCs and can connect you directly with a loan officer at no cost to you.

Can I schedule a site visit before booking?+

Absolutely. Site visits can be booked directly from any property page or by calling our office.

What documents do I need to register a plot?+

Our legal team provides a checklist tailored to your purchase and handles registration paperwork with you.

Do you buy back or resell properties?+

Yes, our resale desk helps existing clients list and sell properties bought through Royal Properties.

What areas do you operate in?+

Primarily Kanchipuram, Sriperumbudur, Walajabad, Uthiramerur and the wider Chennai–Bengaluru growth corridor.

How is pricing decided on listings?+

Prices reflect current market rates for the location and approvals, reviewed periodically against recent registered transactions nearby.

Can NRIs buy property through Royal Properties?+

Yes, we regularly assist NRI clients with remote documentation, power of attorney arrangements and registration support.

Still have a question?

Call, WhatsApp or send us a message — we usually reply within a few hours.
